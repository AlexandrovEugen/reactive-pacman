About dinoman.io

7/19/18

What is dinoman.io?

Dinoman.io is a Pac-Man inspired game in an enclosed maze. Players spawn in as dinos or ghosts and compete to climb the leaderboard. Dinos must eat food and avoid ghosts to survive and gain points. Ghosts must track down dinos with their compass and catch them to gain points. If a dino eats a powerup, dinos are able to eliminate ghosts for a brief period. Ghosts are faster than dinos, so watch out!
